## 📌 Business Problem

The retail industry loses **$1.75 trillion annually** due to inventory
distortion — stockouts and overstocks — while 58% of retailers operate
with inventory accuracy below 80% (IHL Group, 2024). Poor data visibility
forces management into reactive decisions, siloed reporting, and missed
revenue at scale.

## 💡 Solution

I engineered **RetailPulse 360**, an end-to-end Business Intelligence
platform simulating a 12-store Indian retail chain across 5 cities,
designed to eliminate data silos and convert raw operational data into
executive-grade decisions.
